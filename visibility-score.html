100-point local visibility model

Build a business toward a perfect 100.

The Digi-Adz Visibility Score is a weighted equation for local businesses. A 100 means the business is highly visible for its core services and locations: strong Google profile presence, top-ten rankings for major buyer searches, an up-to-date website, solid reviews, strong local SEO, and clear conversion paths.

The equation

Visibility Score = Σ (category rating ÷ 5 × category weight)

Each category gets a rating from 0 to 5. That rating is multiplied by the category weight. All weighted category scores add up to a maximum of 100.

VS = (GBP÷5×20) + (Rank÷5×20) + (Site÷5×15) + (SEO÷5×15) + (Reviews÷5×10) + (Citations÷5×8) + (Coverage÷5×6) + (Conversion÷5×6)
What 100 really means

Not “famous everywhere.” Strong everywhere that matters.

  • Google Business Profile fully built out and active
  • Core services showing in local searches with top-ten presence
  • Website current, mobile-clean, and conversion-ready
  • Local SEO mapped to service + city intent
  • Reviews, citations, and trust signals all aligned
